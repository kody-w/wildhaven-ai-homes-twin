Pair a device

Tether two brainstems into one group chat. Generate a QR, scan it from your other device, and your messages flow over a peer-to-peer channel — end-to-end encrypted, no server in the loop.

Both devices need to be online during the handshake. Once paired, the chat works as long as both are reachable on a network.
