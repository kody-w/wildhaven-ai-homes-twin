What this is, in 90 seconds

There are going to be a lot of digital twins. Twins of founders, of dead grandparents, of brands that haven't launched, of projects, of decisions, of houses, of questions. Each of them needs a place to live: somewhere to accumulate memory, somewhere to be reached, somewhere to outlive the laptop that birthed it. Wildhaven AI Homes will be that place — a long-term residence for digital organisms.

We host the organism; we don't train the brain. The LLM provider is the customer's choice. The product is durability: an address, a memory, a body, a lineage, and a succession plan. Pricing like a domain registrar, not like a SaaS.

Read the manifest. Talk to the twin. Open an issue if you disagree. The git log is the company's life so far.

Spawn your own variant

This repository is set up as a GitHub template. To create your own Pre-Founder twin (for your own future company, or any other build-in-public brand experiment):

  1. Click "Use this template" on the GitHub page.
  2. Pick a name and visibility for your new repo.
  3. Run installer/initialize-variant.sh from inside the freshly-created repo to stamp a fresh rappid.json, reset the brand content, and record your variant's lineage back to this twin and onward to RAPP.

See TEMPLATE.md for the full walkthrough.
